Housing Your Rabbits

Basic Hutch Design

Simple and effective.

A basic hutch (1.4m x 0.6m) houses 1-3 rabbits. Use wire mesh for ventilation and wood for durability.

  • Cost: ~R1500-2000.
  • Materials: Wood, wire mesh, nails.
  • DIY Hutch Guide

Tiered System

Space-saving for multiple rabbits.

Stack 2-3 hutches vertically to house more rabbits efficiently.

  • Cost: ~R3000-5000.
  • Saves land space.
  • Hutch Video Guide

Hutch with Run

Exercise and grazing space.

Attach a run (2m x 1m) for rabbits to move freely and graze safely.

  • Cost: ~R2000-2500.
  • Promotes rabbit health.
  • Housing Tips

Day-to-Day Rabbit Care

Feeding & Nutrition

Daily diet essentials.

Provide hay, pellets (R150/25kg), and fresh greens daily. Water must be clean and constant.

  • Hay: Unlimited, promotes digestion.
  • Pellets: 100g/day per adult.
  • Feeding Guide

Health & Wellness

Preventative care tips.

Check for signs like lethargy or runny eyes. Vaccinations may cost R100/rabbit annually.

  • Regular vet visits.
  • Monitor teeth and claws.
  • Health Tips

Breeding Cycle

Mating and kit care.

Gestation lasts 28-35 days. Provide nest boxes (R200 each) for kindling.

  • Litter size: 6-10 kits.
  • Wean at 6-8 weeks.
  • Breeding Guide

Hutch Cleaning

Maintain hygiene.

Clean weekly with vinegar solution (R20/liter). Remove waste daily.

  • Prevents disease.
  • Improves rabbit comfort.
  • Cleaning Tips

From Kit to Kitchen: Harvesting & Nutrition

How to Harvest

Harvest at 8-12 weeks for optimal meat quality. Use humane methods.

Steps: Stun, bleed, skin, and dress. Process takes ~30 minutes per rabbit.

  • Tools: Sharp knife, gloves (R100/set).
  • Hygiene: Clean workspace.

Delicious Rabbit Recipes

Roasted Rabbit with Herbs

Classic and easy for beginners.

Ingredients: 1 rabbit (1.5kg, R150), rosemary, garlic, olive oil, salt.

Steps: Preheat oven to 180°C. Rub rabbit with oil, herbs, and garlic. Roast for 1.5 hours.

More Recipes

Hearty Rabbit & Vegetable Stew

Comfort food full of flavor.

Ingredients: 1 rabbit, carrots, potatoes, onions, stock (R50 total).

Steps: Brown rabbit, add vegetables and stock, simmer for 2 hours.
